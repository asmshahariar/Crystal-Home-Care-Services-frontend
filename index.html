Our Vision

To give the highest standard of care with love, compassion and dignity, to enable people to live independently in their own homes with peace and contentment.

We will provide a person-centered care service that promotes independence, dignity, and respect for all our service users.

Coronavirus Policy

Safety is most important to us and we take every step to ensure that our staff and service users are protected from COVID-19. We are always offering you the highest standards of care, but now we are also offering you the highest standards of protection.

All our staff are fully trained in infection control procedures and have access to all necessary PPE. Our staff are regularly tested for COVID-19 and follow strict guidelines to ensure the safety of all service users.

We are proud to announce that our service has remained COVID-19 free since the beginning of the pandemic, thanks to our rigorous infection control protocols.

We can provide a full risk assessment and COVID-19 protection plan for all new service users. Please contact us for more information.
